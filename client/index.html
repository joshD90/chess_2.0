Welcome! This you can play chess either single player, all on the one screen, or you can play online. For the single player you make moves for both white and black pieces. There is an option in the top right of the corner to have the board flip with every turn so you can always see it from that players perspective

Starting an online game is very simple. Open a new browser window at this same url. Now provide your name and select the same time limit on both screens and you will be connected! Or even better yet, send the link to a friend and test your skills against them. Happy Chessing!
